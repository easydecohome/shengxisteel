FAQ

Answers before you order.

What certifications do your products have?
Our steel complies with international standards, including ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ASTM, EN, JIS, GB (Material Standards) and SGS / BV / TÜV third-party inspection, available on request.
How do you ensure product quality during shipping?
Anti-rust coating and waterproof packaging, containerized shipping (20ft / 40ft, FCL & LCL options), plus real-time GPS & humidity tracking for sensitive shipments.
What are your shipping terms?
We support FOB (you arrange shipping from our port), CIF (we handle delivery to your port) and EXW (pickup from our factory). DDP / DAP also negotiable for bulk orders.
How long does production and delivery take?
Standard products: 7–15 days after deposit. Custom orders: 15–45 days depending on complexity. Shipping: 15–40 days, varying by destination.
Can you provide samples?
Yes — free small samples (customer pays shipping), and full-sample batches with cost credited against future orders.
What payment methods do you accept?
T/T (Bank Transfer): 30% deposit, 70% before shipment. L/C: irrevocable at sight. Flexible terms for long-term partners.
